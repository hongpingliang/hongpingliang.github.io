Our dog, Kringle, is almost 12 year old.  We used to have a van with low frame so he can jump up and down easily.  The van got totaled so we had to use a sedan to take him dog park.  The back seat is high and with the old age, it was hard for him to get on.  The first time he was excited and jumped right into the car.  After coming back home, he looked at the height and hesitated, then sat back and did not want to get out
![dog in car](/images/dog_in_car.png)
We spend about twenty minutes with various trick plus treat and eventually he jumped out.  It was a hassee morning since I was hurrying to a meeting.

Later I removed the back seats and placed board to make it even. 
![dog car remove seat](/images/dog_car_remove_seat.png)

With a board and some studs, I also build a loading ramp so can just walk into the car.

However, To let him use the loading ramp was a really challenge.  At the beginning, he did not want anywhere near the stool. Pulling on leash, food did not help. Seems that all the work were wasted. For about a week, we tried everything but could not get him into the car.

So the first step was to get him on the ramp.  I put the ramp at the bottom of the stair from the garage to the house, and blocked the area so he can only step on the ramp to go home.  With bounch of treats, and patience, he could not find other way around and eventually step on the ramp.  Once the first time, with more food, it got easier.  I left the ramp there for a about two weeks, until he seems no hestitaion to step on it.  

Afte that, with the loading ramp, to get to the car is easy.  It is not easy to teach old dog trick.  To see him walk up and down the car without jumping is really amazing!
