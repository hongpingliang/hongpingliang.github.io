I saw bear twice last year during my morning run. 

Once on our neighour's front yard. It was sniffing on a cutted tree trunk and about 30 yards from me. My brain went blank and not sure what I should do, a little scary.  I slowed down and started running to other side of road to get farther away.  The bear saw me and he started to run away into the wood.  My fear went away and I just resumed my running as usual.

The second time was in a raining afternoon.  The bear just reached the side and road and was tring to go across the road. I feeled a little calm this time and was not afraid that much.  I told a person sitting in front of her house waiting for school bus and told her the bear.  She ran out but too late, the bear already crossed the road and disappered into the wood.  I feel bad to tell her but she did not see it.

I did not get any photo both time and wished I had camera with me, I am still looking forward to the third time.
