During my running, sometime I will meet various people saying something interesting. 

Usually, people says "Good moring", "Nice day to run!", "How many miles you run", "What are you training for", "Do you need gel" from a fellow runner, "You are fast", "Your are my hero", "Brave soul" if raining, "I am so sorry" for getting a dog jumping on. "My name is James" from a stopped car

One time, a car slowed down behind me and the driver said "you have nice calf musles, that is not easy to get there".

My favorite one is that a car stopped in front of me, and I slowed down and looked at the driver through the passenger window.  The driver said "I almost missed you".  I was lost and not sure what he mean.  He said it again and added, "you need to run on the other side of road".  I said "Thank you" automatically, but feeled uneasy.  He almost missed to see me, and possible hit me.  From that on, I will wear more bright color and try to run more on the opposite of road.  It is a little hard on the feet if you always run on just one side of road.
