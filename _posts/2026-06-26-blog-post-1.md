We saw many deers while walking dog in the wood.  They are usually far away and the dog sees them before me.  He will jump up, sprint and bark so the deers will just run away. Most of time, the deer will stop at some distance and look back at us with the head twisted and face to us.  Then, the dog will chase again. With two or three rounds, the deer will disappeared eventaully.  

However this young deer seems like us.  He kept following us even being chased and bared several time.  Finally, the deer find a change to sniff the dog
